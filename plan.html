Your 7-Day Movement Plan

Gentle, no-equipment routine to move your body, relax your mind, and care for your heart.

🌿 Monday

Gentle Mobility + Breathing

  • 5–10 min joint rotations
  • Shoulder, hip, knee circles
  • 5 min deep breathing

❤️ Tuesday

Low-Impact Cardio Walk

  • 15–20 min brisk walk
  • Steady breathing
  • 5 min stretch after

🧘‍♀️ Wednesday

Beginner Yoga / Stretch

  • 10–15 min gentle flow
  • Cat-Cow, Child’s Pose
  • Supine Twist, relaxation

💙 Thursday

Light Cardio + Core

  • 10 min easy moves
  • March, knee raises
  • 5 min planks (knees ok)

🌸 Friday

Mindfulness + Breathwork

  • 5 min box breathing
  • 5 min body scan
  • Optional journaling

💪 Saturday

Simple Bodyweight Strength

  • 3 rounds:
  • 10 squats, push-ups, bridges
  • 5 min stretch after

🌿 Sunday

Active Recovery + Gratitude

  • 10–20 min slow walk
  • Gentle seated stretching
  • 3 things you're grateful for
